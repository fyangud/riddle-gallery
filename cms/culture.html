Event Preview

The First Full Moon Festival, which falls on the 15th of the Lunar calendar, known also as Yuanxiao in China and Daeboreum in Korea, is an important part of the celebration of the Lunar New Year in East Asia.

We've prepared for you various activities both in traditional festive & normal Sasaki Social event format. We will provide food and drinks from across East Asia that are used to celebrate the festival, such as sweet rice balls, dumplings, porridge, and traditional snacks. And the final part of our event includes games and DIY crafts activities.

Here's a sneak peek of what you could expect during the event:

Games:

● Traditional games such as solving lantern riddles (share your riddles using the link at the end of this mailer!), pitch-pot (throwing arrows into the canisters), lucky red bags and Bureom (breaking nuts for good luck).

Crafts:

● Learn how to craft origami bokjori(a dipper shaped lucky bag), paper bunnies & traditional paper cutting, and explore your creativities through lantern doodles.
● A specially decorated photo booth featuring a festival feast collage to keep your memories.

Food:

● Enjoy Yuanxiao(sweet rice balls), Boreuma(a collection of various kinds of nuts such as peanuts, walnuts, pine nuts, chestnuts, and gingko nuts) and many other delicious treats.
